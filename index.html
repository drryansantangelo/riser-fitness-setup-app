Step 3 of 3

Set Up Your Riser Music Player

Sonos + Mobile App

What You'll Need to Complete This Setup

The Sonos app installed on your mobile device.
A Sonos system already set up and connected to Wi-Fi.
A Soundtrack Your Brand account with an active subscription.
Your Soundtrack username and password.

Provided to you by your system administrator.

Connect Soundtrack to Your Sonos System

1

Open Sonos

Launch the Sonos app on your mobile device.

2

Add Soundtrack as a Music Service

  • In Your Services, tap the "+" icon.
  • Scroll down and select Soundtrack (red icon).
  • Tap Add to Sonos.
3

Authorize Soundtrack

  • On the Add Sonos Account screen, tap Authorize.
  • Then tap Login and continue setup.
4

Log In to Soundtrack

  • Enter the Soundtrack credentials provided by your system administrator.
  • Tap Login.
5

Select Your Location

  • Choose your location from the list.
  • When prompted, tap Open to return to the Sonos app.
6

Confirm & Complete Setup

  • Confirm the correct location is shown at the top.
  • Tap Continue.
  • Tap Use Soundtrack as your preferred service.
  • Tap Done.

Sonos + Desktop App

What You'll Need to Complete This Setup

The Sonos app installed on your computer (PC or Mac).
A Sonos system already set up and connected to Wi-Fi or Ethernet.
Your computer connected to the same network as your Sonos system.

Provided to you by your system administrator.

Set Up Soundtrack in the Sonos Desktop App

1

Open the Sonos Desktop App

Launch the Sonos app on your Windows PC or Mac.

2

Add Soundtrack as a Music Service

  • In the left menu, open Services & Voice.
  • Click Add a Service.
  • Scroll to find Soundtrack (red icon).
  • Click Add to Sonos.
3

Authorize Soundtrack

  • A window will open prompting you to authorize.
  • Click Authorize.
4

Log In to Soundtrack

  • Click Login and continue setup.
  • Enter your Soundtrack username and password.
  • Click Login.
5

Select Your Location

Choose your correct Riser Fitness location from the list and confirm.

6

Complete Setup

  • When prompted, click Open Sonos to return to the app.
  • Confirm the location is correct.
  • Click Continue, then click Done.

Set Up Your Riser Music Player

Mobile Device

Recommended Setup

Turn your phone or tablet into the Riser music player by installing the Soundtrack Player app and connecting it to your existing audio system.

Setup Steps

  1. 1
    Connect your device to your sound system

    Ensure your phone/tablet is physically connected to your amp/speakers via audio cable or Bluetooth.

  2. 2
    Download the Soundtrack Player app
  3. 3
    Open the app and log in

    Use the Riser Fitness Soundtrack credentials provided to you. Need your login info?

  4. 4
    Confirm Wi-Fi connection

    Make sure your device is connected to a stable Wi-Fi network for uninterrupted playback.

  5. 5
    Adjust device settings

    Prevent interruptions by:

    • Disabling auto-lock / setting screen timeout to "Never"
    • Enabling Do Not Disturb mode
    • Keeping the device plugged in to power
  6. 6
    Start playing your Riser playlist

    Select the approved Riser playlist and set the volume to a comfortable level for your space.

Staff can use the Soundtrack Remote Control app on their personal phones to change playlists, skip tracks, and adjust music without touching the main player device.

How to Set Up Remote Control

  • Download the Remote app:
    iOS App Store Google Play
  • Log in with the same Riser Fitness Soundtrack credentials.
  • Select your location from the list to connect to the player.
  • Control playback: Change playlists, skip tracks, pause, or adjust volume.

Set Up Your Riser Music Player

PlayerOne Device

Recommended Setup

Replace your existing hardware music box with a preconfigured PlayerOne device for Riser Fitness. PlayerOne is the easiest and most reliable way to play Soundtrack music.

Order PlayerOne

PlayerOne is a dedicated Soundtrack device that comes pre-configured for Riser Fitness. Once it arrives, setup takes just minutes.

Order PlayerOne

Once your PlayerOne arrives, follow the setup steps below.

Setup Steps

  1. 1
    Unbox PlayerOne

    Remove PlayerOne from the packaging and place it near your amp/receiver and router.

  2. 2
    Connect audio output

    Use the included audio cable to connect PlayerOne's audio output to your amp/speakers.

  3. 3
    Connect to power and internet

    Plug in the power adapter. For best results, connect to your router via Ethernet cable. Wi-Fi setup is also available.

  4. 4
    Power on and wait for auto-configuration

    PlayerOne will automatically load the Riser Fitness configuration. The LED will turn solid when ready (usually 1-2 minutes).

  5. 5
    Test playback

    Music should start playing automatically. Adjust the volume on your amp to a comfortable level.
